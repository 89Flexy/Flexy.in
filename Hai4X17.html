Haikyu!!

[ Season 4 ]

Overview

Haikyu!! follows Shōyō Hinata, a determined volleyball player inspired by a short pro known as “The Little Giant.” Despite his height, Hinata’s energy and drive push him to form a strong team and face rivals like Kageyama — his former opponent turned teammate. The anime celebrates teamwork, perseverance, and the thrill of sports.

Genre: Sports, Comedy, Drama

Language: Hindi, Japanese, English
