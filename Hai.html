Haikyu!!

[4 Season || 25 Episodes]

Overview

Haikyuu!! follows Shōyō Hinata, a short but energetic boy who dreams of becoming a top volleyball player despite his height. Inspired by a player called “The Little Giant,” he revives his middle school’s volleyball club — only to be crushed by the genius setter Tobio Kageyama in their first match. But fate brings them together in high school — both joining Karasuno High’s volleyball team. Forced to work as teammates, Hinata’s agility and Kageyama’s precision form an unbeatable duo. Together, they aim to lead Karasuno back to national glory.

Genre: School life, Comedy, Sports

Languages: Japanese, Hindi, English
